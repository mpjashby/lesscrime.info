--- title: "Chart: Almost 7 million adults have been victims of partner abuse" author: Matt Ashby date: '2019-08-26' slug: partner-abuse categories: ["Crime and justice chart of the week"] tags: - crime - violence - domestic abuse - CSEW ---

Among the 42 million adults in England and Wales aged between 16 and 74, 1.3 million have been sexually assaulted by a current or former partner, 4.3 million subjected to non-sexual assault and 2.3 million have been stalked. In every category, at least twice as many women as men have been victimised.
