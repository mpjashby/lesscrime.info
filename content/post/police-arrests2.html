--- title: "Chart: Police are arresting half as many people as a decade ago" author: 'Matt Ashby' date: '2019-10-14' categories: ["Crime and justice chart of the week"] tags: - police - arrests - criminal justice - trends ---

A combination of legal changes and decreases in many common types of crime, together with closures of custody suites due to budget cuts, mean police are making about 760,000 fewer arrests per year now, compared to a decade ago. The number of children arrested has decreased fastest, potentially driven by recognition of the negative consequences of criminalising young people.
