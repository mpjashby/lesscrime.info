Chart: Homicides haven't only increased in Britain

January 2020 1 min read Crime and justice chart of the week

The homicide-rate increase over the past five years hasn’t been unique to England and Wales, with murders also increasing in France, Germany and Sweden after continent-wide decreases since the 1990s. The UK-wide homicide rate remains about average for European countries.
